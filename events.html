UPCOMING EVENTS

EVENT: ABSTRACT ART SHOWCASE

In this event, presenters share and explain their abstract pieces. They will each have a 15 minute time slot to talk (although they can end any time they want). This event was created in order to people realize the beauty of abstract art.

EVENT: SKETCH COMPETITION

This event will be a competition with 3 rounds. Each round, participants are given 20 minutes to complete a sketch according to a theme given by judges. After each round, participants submit their sketch to be graded. Whoever receives the highest grade from the judges is the winner of the competition!

EVENT: PAINTING GALLERY

In this event, we will be viewing and appreciating user-submitted paintings. After this, there will be a silent auction in which paintings can be bought.
