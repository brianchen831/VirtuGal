Topic: Tickets

Published 4/12/2022 by Flacia Nu

VirtuGal has truly helped me further kindle my love for the large array of arts during this pandemic. Having limited amounts of in-person experiences to visit art galleries and expositions, provides a seamless transition to connect with others online. As a long-time user, I can assure any individuals new to this incredible platform that they have nothing to worry about regarding tickets and admission of any sort. Not once have I received any subpar performance from VirtuGal in transactions. It's a simple process and is streamlined as you view the events you are interested in attending. Once you choose the given event, a clear “Buy Tickets” button is displayed. You’d choose the number of tickets you are interested in buying. On the same page, all the information is provided to the event as to what the event includes and its costs. You continue by entering your personal email and billing information. Once the transaction has gone through, a confirmation email is sent to you with your ticketing information. It's as easy as that! Would 100% recommend.
