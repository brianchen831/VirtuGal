3/28/22

Event: Origami Showcase

Origami has long been a part of many people’s lives. Folding paper cranes were an integral part of our childhood, and making origami frogs distracted from paying attention in math class. For many of us, it has been quite a while since we last engaged in this particular craft. Virtugal hosted an event featuring a series of origami tutorials - cranes, boxes, dogs, and more - to help people reconnect with their fondest childhood memories whilst making adorable additions to their desk decorations.

3/28/22

Clay Competition

In the past few months, we have seen an increased interest in ceramics (especially air dry clay) take the art world by storm. This new obsession with clay has proved to be an excellent way to engage with all groups of people; those with no formal background in art are able to enjoy themselves while creating small figurines and trinkets out of clay, and those who are not able to dedicate a large amount of financial resources are not left out either due to the low, inclusive price of air dry clay. Virtugal’s clay competition was a rousing success because so many people chose to participate. Our winner, ultimately, was the bull-shaped bell due to its creativity and skillful craftsmanship.
