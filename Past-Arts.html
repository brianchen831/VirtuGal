Theme: The Art In Mathematics

Led By: Arthur Brown
Number of Attendees: 157
Duration of Event: 17:32

About Me

Arthur Brown

I was raised in a small town on the outskirts of Alabama. Growing up, I was always interested in numbers and how they could make such beautiful things when used in relation with each other. What really sparked my curiosity were shapes. I love how every thing that we perseve on this earth can be broken up into shapes and into mathematics. In this event, I tried explain to my audience more about how art and math are more similar than one normally think. I loved this experience, and I hope to hold an event just like this one again sometime soon!
