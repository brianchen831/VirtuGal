Topic: Free Events

Published 4/27/2022 by Ethan McMac

I think many people, especially teens, are not participating since they don’t want to pay the required fee that comes with the event. 10 dollars or so might not seem like much to most people, but that adds up when you’re going with friends and family. The financial barrier discourages many less fortunate people to join in on the fun, as they are unsure if their money is being spent wisely. That’s why we should host free events (maybe even as a one-time thing so people will know what the events are like). Of course, I believe there are some negative drawbacks to free events, so I decided to make a pros and cons list below.

Pros:
More accessible to everyone
More rewards are available relative to the number of competitors present
Larger pools of competitors
Less fortunate people can easily participate
Gives more recognition to newer artists and hosts
Cons:
Lower quality events
Relies on fundraising and volunteering
