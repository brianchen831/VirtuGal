ABOUT

VirtuGal was founded in 2021 and was created to make up for the inability for people to attend in person events. It is an art gallery with pieces of all styles: our goal is to connect different people through art after the pandemic split us apart.

EVENTS

VirtuGal holds community-led events weekly. Each topic is chosen by the community. If you wish, you can also host your own event. Plan a date, time, and theme, and we will ensure that you are able to spread your interests to the others around the world.

COMMUNITY

VirtuGal has created a community built upon learning from others and sharing ideas. Take a look through an article, discuss your thoughts with others, submit an artwork and get feedback, or watch an event held completely online. The world of art is in your hands.
