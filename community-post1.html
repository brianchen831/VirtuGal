Topic: Upcoming Events

Published 4/7/2022 by User

Virtugal is proud to announce three exciting events coming your way this summer. If you’re eager to get creative but not so eager about the heat, we encourage you to partake in these completely virtual events to satisfy both desires: get your craft on from the comfort of your own home!

July 1st, 2022 at 10am

Grab your pencils and get ready to sketch! This sketching competition is designed to challenge artists’ creativity and allow for increased engagement with each competitor’s unique styles. The competition will feature three rounds in which participants will be given 20 minutes to complete a sketch to the best of their ability that fits within the theme selected by the judges. After sketches have been submitted virtually and the judge panel has taken the time to evaluate each drawing, the lucky winner will be awarded with a prize! No more spoilers: attend to learn more!

July 3rd, 2022 at 4pm

This showcase is a wonderful opportunity for artists who seek to share their abstract pieces to meet viewers who seek to learn more about the world of abstract art. Because abstract art is often denounced as having “no meaning” and perceived to be easily replicable by just anyone, this event is designed to shed light on the beauty of abstract art by allowing artists to share the messages and motivations behind their works.

July 5th, 2022 at 2pm

Painters, listen up! Virtugal’s online painting gallery is the perfect chance for you to submit your art to be showcased as part of a virtual gallery. Add your paintings to the collection and hop online to explore and take inspiration from the works of other artists. The event will culminate in the form of a silent auction in which buyers will have the chance to purchase your original paintings. Not only will this be a great opportunity to earn some cash, but it will also give you a venue to converse with artists and buyers alike and share the meaning behind your artwork!
