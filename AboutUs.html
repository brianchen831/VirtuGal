OUR MISSION

During the pandemic, quarantine separated us from loved ones and prevented us from expressing ourselves. Art is a timeless way in which we can express ourselves and step into an immersive experience filled with wonder.

VirtuGal's mission is to cultivate an environment for people to connect, share, and explore artistic creations.

OUR STORY

  • Covid's Impact

    January 2021

    After Covid had hit, lockdowns and a loss in jobs made it hard for artists to get their work shown. This turn of events eliminated opportunities for people across the world to experience cultural ideas and expression.

  • Our Journey Begins

    August 2021

    Connected by our interests in art and technology, we high schoolers decided to start a non-profit project called Virtugal that, at its core, can help to mitigate the impact Covid has had on the art industry.

  • The First Step

    January 2022

    Our efforts bore fruit, as the website started kicking off with artists worldwide hosting events with hundreds of viewers. To make our platform more interactive, we added a way for users to make their own events simpler and added a community page allow for users to speak with each other in an easier manner.

  • Partnerships

    February 2022

    We decided to collaborate with The Dreaming Zebra Foundation to help children across the world. Our cooperation has featured allowed for many new events, further helping artists to share their artworks and gain recognition in times of recovery.

  • Moving Forward

    April 2022

    As we expand our boundaries and reach a larger audience, we hope to inspire and motivate others to keep posting their creative artworks online while featuring unique art events for younger artists starting out.

Events

VirtuGal holds community-led events weekly. The topics discussed in each event are changed every time and depend on what you want to see. If you wish, you can also host your own event. Plan a date, time, and theme, and we will ensure that you are able to spread your interests to the community.

For more information and to view what is currently available, head to the events page!
